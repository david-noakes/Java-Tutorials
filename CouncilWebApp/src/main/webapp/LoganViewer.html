Welcome

The Planning Viewer provides public access to the map layers for certain planning maps incorporated by reference in environmental planning instruments made under the Environmental Planning and Assessment Act 1979.

The Viewer is an initiative of the New South Wales Government and is designed to help people better understand map-based planning information.

The Viewer enables users to search, navigate and view the map layers using standard map viewer functionality.

The service is maintained by the Department of Planning and Environment.

Planning Viewer Terms and Conditions of use

The Planning Viewer provides public access to the map layers for certain planning maps incorporated by reference in environmental planning instruments made under the Environmental Planning and Assessment Act 1979.
The Planning Viewer enables users to search, navigate and view the map layers using standard map viewer functionality.

PDF versions of the official maps adopted when the instruments are made are available from a map link index from the relevant environmental planning instrument on the NSW legislation website.

The Planning Viewer is an initiative of the New South Wales Government and has been designed to help people better understand map-based planning information. The service is maintained by the Department of Planning and Environment.

Access and use of the Planning Viewer

The NSW Government grants You a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to access and use the Planning Viewer in accordance with these terms and conditions.

The Planning Viewer beta program

The Planning Viewer is being made publicly available as part of an open beta program. The purpose of the program is to make a pre-release version of the Planning Viewer available to the public for the purpose of providing feedback on the quality and usability of the service, including the map layers.

Map layers

The map layers available in the Planning Viewer should be treated as being reference documents only and You should exercise Your own skill and care with respect to the use of the material.

The map layers are updated as new PDF maps are published. You should be aware that there may be delays between when the official PDF maps are published on the NSW legislation website and when the map layers are updated on the Planning Viewer. The Department of Planning and Environment tries to update the layers within 2 weeks of any changes to the official PDF maps.

The Planning Viewer only shows the current In Force map layers. Historical versions of the maps are available from a map link index from the relevant environmental planning instrument on the NSW legislation website.
